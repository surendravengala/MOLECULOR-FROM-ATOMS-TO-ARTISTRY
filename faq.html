Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

What is Moleculor?

Moleculor is an innovative e-commerce platform that combines cutting-edge molecular technology with fashion and lifestyle products, offering sustainable and high-quality solutions for modern shoppers.

How can I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of our homepage and fill in the required details, such as your name, email address, and password, to create your account.

Orders and Shipping

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, net banking, and popular payment wallets. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location. Typically, orders are delivered within 5-7 business days. You will receive a tracking link once your order is shipped.

Returns and Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Products must be unused and in their original packaging. Please visit our Returns page for more details.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, contact our support team at support@moleculor.com with your order details. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

Customer Support

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at support@moleculor.com or call our helpline at +1-800-555-1234. Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

Do you offer live chat support?

Yes, we offer live chat support on our website during business hours. Click on the chat icon at the bottom right corner to start a conversation.
